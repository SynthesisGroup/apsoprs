Travel Grant

The Asia-Pacific Society of Ophthalmic Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery (APSOPRS) was established in 2000 aiming to assist the development of Ophthalmic Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery in the Asia-Pacific region and provide a platform for good integration of skills and knowledge of clinicians in this subspecialty.

We are pleased to offer up to 10 APSOPRS Travel Grants for ophthalmologists in the Asia-Pacific region. Please click “Apply for APSOPRS Travel Grant” in the abstract submission page to apply, and the committee will select recipients according to the content of the abstracts.

Priority will be given to ophthalmologists who are:

  1. From the APSOPRS member countries.
  2. Whose presentations (oral communication or poster) have been accepted by the Scientific Program Committee of the APSOPRS 2023.
  3. International transportation costs only.

Successful applicant will receive US$500 from the APSOPRS 2023.
