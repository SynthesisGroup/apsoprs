Tivoli Chengdu at Cultural Heritage Park

No. 601-1 Guanghua Avenue, International Intangible Cultural Heritage Expo Park, Qingyang District, Chengdu.

Book Now
Chengdu Shuangliu International Airport: 25 minutes - by car
Chengdu South Railway Station: 35 minutes - by car
Chengdu West Railway Station: 14 minutes - by car
Subway line 4 Intangible Cultural Heritage Station: 2 minutes - walking

The hotel is located in the International Intangible Cultural Heritage Park, Guanghua Avenue. It is adjacent to the Qingyang Oasis Headquarters Economic Industrial Park and the Qingyang District Government Affairs Center. It is only a short walk from the Intangible Cultural Heritage Expo Park on Metro Line 4 and provides easy and direct access to several attractive locations in the city, including Kuanzhai Alley and Dufu Thatched Cottage. The hotel is accessible in all directions, 25 minutes' drive from Shuangliu Airport, and adjacent to Guanghua Avenue, the Ring Expressway, the Chengdu-Wenzhou-Qionglai Expressway, and other major traffic arteries. It is very convenient to drive to Dujiangyan, Qingcheng Mountain, Emei Mountain, and other scenic spots.

Travel Information

Chengdu is a beautiful place, is one of the most important economic centres, transportation and communication hubs located in Sichuan Province. The city is exquisitely decorated and well-designed, has become an important joining point to the world heritage tour in western China. Qingcheng Mountain is a famous mountain of Taoism, surrounded by countless peaks and densely covered by ancient trees. Dujiangyan Irrigation Project is a great project with great fame in the world, with a history over 2000 years. Both are within one hourâ€™s drive.

Chengdu is the home town of giant pandas. The Research Breeding Base of Giant Pandas is the only wild animal breeding base in urban area in the world. Perennial here with more than 20 captive giant pandas and lesser panda, black-necked cranes, herons and other rare animals. Black and white with an air of charming naivety the giant pandas win the favor of people around the world with their lovely image. Here is the best place to watch, be close to, and explore the growth mysteries of the giant pandas.
