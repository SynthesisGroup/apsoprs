Abstract Submission

We are inviting our colleagues to submit abstracts for oral and poster presentations for the Asia Pacific Society of Ophthalmic Plastic and Reconstructive Surgery (APSOPRS) Biennial Meeting in Chengdu.

All abstracts must be written in English, with a maximum length of 300 words. Where appropriate, abstract headings should read: background or purpose; methods; results; and conclusions.

Please see the guidelines below:

  • If there are multiple authors, the primary author is responsible for securing consent for submission from his co-authors.
  • The primary author is responsible for securing consent from patients whose clinical data will be included in the final presentation.
  • There is no limit to the number of submissions per author.
  • Abstracts should be submitted before April 15, 2023.
  • Primary authors of accepted presentations will be informed via email.
  • Please click on the button to open the submission form. Once the abstracts are submitted, the content cannot be changed.

Authors will be informed by email if their submissions are accepted. Prizes will be awarded for the best entries for each category.
